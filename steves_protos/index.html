The Next Housing Crisis

In this final project, we look at several factors that we believe could contribute to the next housing crisis. We'll review each one and provide a narrative as to how and why we think this factor will contribute to a future housing crisis. What we'll cover:
  • A look back at the last housing crisis
  • Demographic changes
  • Student loan debt
  • Consumer credit debt
  • Fluctuating interest rates
  • Foreign Investment in Real Estate
  • Economic Trends
We'll close with a summary of when we think the next hosuing crisis will hit.
